# The Coinspiratorium
*Fine print. Full price.*

## Overview
The Coinspiratorium is a magic item shop of dubious reputation and undeniable inventory, located in [[Offledger]] — Veezy's extradimensional criminal haven. It serves as the players' home base, primary source of income, and the front operation for [[Vizzok the Opulent|Lord Veezy's]] criminal enterprise. The players run it, stock it, defend it, and profit from it — in that order of priority.

The shop's clientele is drawn from Offledger's population of criminals, outcasts, planar drifters, and esoteric wanderers — the kind of people who need magic items and don't ask where they came from.

## Notable Features
- **The Showroom** — where customers browse merchandise
- **The Back Room** — where deals are made and contracts are assigned
- **The Vault** — where the really valuable (and really dangerous) stock is kept
- **Living Quarters** — the players have to sleep somewhere
- **The Ledger** — a magically-binding record of all transactions and debts

## Key NPCs
- [[Vizzok the Opulent]] — owner (you just work here)
- [[The Eyecons]] — Veezy's entourage, frequent visitors and handlers
- *Players* — involuntary proprietors

## Services Offered
- Magical items for sale (at exorbitant prices)
- Obscure curiosities
- Minor cataclysms (on request)
- Promotional hexes
- Item identification (for a fee, naturally)
- "Returns" (store credit in another dimension)

## Hooks & Opportunities
- Customers bring plot hooks through the door
- Inventory management creates resource tension (stock the shop vs. keep gear vs. pay debt)
- The shop can be upgraded, expanded, or threatened
- Rival shops, regulatory bodies, or thieves create external pressure
- Veezy's agents stop by to collect tribute and assign contracts

## The Endgame Role
The Coinspiratorium counter is where the [[Veezy's IOU|final transaction]] takes place. When the PCs place Veezy's IOU on the counter, the shop becomes the instrument of his downfall. The store they were forced to run becomes the weapon that sets them free.
