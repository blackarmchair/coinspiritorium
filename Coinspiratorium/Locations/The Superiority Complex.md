_Headquarters of O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E._

Suspended in the Astral Sea at the intersection of seven stabilized ley lines, the **Superiority Complex** rises in immaculate tiers of white stone and mirrored adamantine. From a distance, the structure resembles a perfectly symmetrical cube — restrained and mathematically inevitable. No banners hang from its walls. No sigils advertise its presence. It does not need to.

The Complex is composed of concentric administrative layers arranged around a central Adjudication Spire. Each ring houses a division of O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E., from Artifact Reclamation to Interplanar Extradition. Hallways are geometrically precise, their angles exact to the inch. Illusions and teleportation effects function within strictly defined parameters; unauthorized conjurations simply fail. Visitors often report an uncomfortable sensation upon entry — as though their posture has been corrected without their consent.

At the heart of the structure lies the Grand Tribunal, a cathedral-like chamber of cold light and flawless acoustics. Here, indictments are read, warrants authorized, and entire demiplanes placed under injunction. When a judgment is rendered, the sound resonates through the Complex like a gavel struck against the multiverse itself.

Agents refer to their headquarters simply as “the Complex.” Outsiders rarely use the name twice.

**Traits:** Strong lawful aura; enhanced divination effects (caster level +2 within the structure); teleportation and planar travel require authorization sigils; morale penalties (–1 on Bluff checks) for creatures attempting deception within the central rings.

**Symbol:** A balanced scale superimposed over a stylized spire.

The Superiority Complex stands in deliberate contrast to Offledger’s improvisational chaos. Where Vizzok the Opulent builds power through ego and entropy, O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. builds inevitability through structure. The Complex does not pursue criminals in haste. It waits. It documents. It prepares.

And when it moves, it does so from a position of absolute certainty.
