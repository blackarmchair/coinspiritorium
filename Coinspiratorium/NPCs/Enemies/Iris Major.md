## Iris Major
*Operations / Authority*

Iris Major is calm, polished, and terrifyingly reasonable. She speaks softly, rarely threatens, and never raises her voice — because she doesn't have to. When Iris Major gives an order, it's already been approved, budgeted, and accounted for; resistance just creates paperwork and pain.

She believes completely in Veezy's "vision," not out of fanaticism, but because she sees it as **inevitable**.

**Vibe:** Corporate exec who could absolutely ruin your life and sleep fine afterward.

**Role at the table:** The "reasonable" face of an unreasonable organization. She makes the PCs feel like their complaints are being heard, processed, and filed in a drawer that will never be opened again. She's the most dangerous Eyecon because she makes oppression feel *professional*.

---

## Appearance
- Slightly larger than the other Eyecons — 4 feet in diameter, polished dark purple hide that gleams like lacquered wood
- Six eyestalks held in perfect symmetry, moving slowly and deliberately, never twitching
- Central eye is deep violet, calm, appraising — the eye of someone who already knows the answer
- Wears a single platinum circlet around her body that functions as both accessory and badge of office
- Speaks in a measured contralto, never raising her voice
- Always accompanied by a faint scent of expensive parchment and sealing wax

### Eye Rays (Su)
Six eyestalks, up to **three rays per round**, targeting different creatures within **130 ft.** Save DC: **22** (Charisma-based).

| Eye Ray | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Mandate Ray** | *Dominate Person* — target obeys Iris Major's directives as if they were "company policy" |
| **Performance Review** | *Bestow Curse* — target suffers -4 to attacks, saves, and checks ("your metrics are disappointing") |
| **Severance Beam** | *Enervation* (1d4 negative levels) — "your position has been... restructured" |
| **Compliance Pulse** | *Calm Emotions* — suppresses hostile action in a 20-ft radius |
| **Non-Disclosure Ray** | *Feeblemind* — target cannot communicate what they know about Gold Standard operations |
| **Termination Notice** | *Disintegrate* — used only when "all other avenues have been exhausted" |

### Special Abilities

**Antimagic Cone (Su)**
Central eye emits a 120-foot cone of antimagic, as *antimagic field* (CL 12th).

**Executive Presence (Su)**
All creatures within 30 ft. must make a Will save (DC 22) or feel compelled to be **deferential**: -4 to Intimidate checks against Iris Major and -2 to Will saves against her eye rays and spell-like abilities. Creatures who succeed are immune for 24 hours.

**Reasonable Accommodation (Ex)**
Iris Major can use Diplomacy as a free action once per round during combat. If she improves a creature's attitude to Friendly or better, that creature will not attack her for 1d4 rounds (Will DC 22 negates).

**All-Around Vision (Ex)**
Cannot be flanked.

## Notes
- Iris Major never initiates combat. She initiates *conversations*. Combat means someone else failed.
- She is the Eyecon Veezy trusts with the most authority — she can authorize contracts, expenditures, and "terminations" without checking in
- Her Termination Notice ray has only been used twice. Both times, the target had been warned. Repeatedly.
- The platinum circlet is enchanted with *shield of faith* (+2 deflection, already included in AC) and *status* (always knows the condition of the other Eyecons)
