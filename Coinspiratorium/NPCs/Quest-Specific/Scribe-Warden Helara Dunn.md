# Scribe-Warden Helara Dunn

Human Scribe-Warden at Blackstaff Tower. Handles audit authorization from her 2nd-floor office and personally controls access to the 3rd-floor Vault containing the Black Ledger. The party's single bureaucratic antagonist in Quest 04 — they must get past her twice (once for paperwork, once for vault access).
