## Snitch Ray
*Surveillance / Informants*

Snitch Ray is a bitter, joyless professional whose entire personality is "documenting your failure." He is constantly watching, constantly reporting, and constantly framing events in the least charitable way possible.

Even Veezy finds him annoying, but keeps him around because Snitch Ray is **always right** — and always loyal to power, not people. The PCs should feel like he's narcing on them even when they do nothing wrong.

**Vibe:** Internal affairs investigator who hates his own side more than the enemy.

**Role at the table:** Paranoia fuel. Every time the PCs do something shady, Snitch Ray saw it. Every time they cut a corner, Snitch Ray documented it. He's the reason they can't relax.
