# Purpose

Defines a repeatable structure for generating **Coinspiratorium-style dungeons**.

Dungeons in this campaign are **thematic artifact vaults**, not generic monster caves.

---

# Dungeon Generation Framework

Generate the following elements.

```
Dungeon Theme
Original Owner
Reason It Exists
Artifact Stored Inside
Environmental Identity
Guardians
Unique Mechanics
Dungeon Twist
```

---

# Dungeon Themes

Themes should be strong and unusual.

Examples:

* dragon hoard vault
* celestial archive
* cursed theater
* time-loop prison
* planar museum
* magical casino

Avoid generic fantasy ruins.

---

# Original Owner

Every dungeon once belonged to someone.

Examples:

* archmage
* dragon
* celestial order
* lich collector
* ancient empire

This determines the dungeon style.

---

# Reason It Exists

Why was this place created?

Examples:

* vault for powerful artifacts
* prison for magical beings
* research facility
* divine trial chamber

---

# Artifact

Every dungeon revolves around an artifact.

Artifacts should:

* be narratively significant
* have unusual magical properties
* be desirable to Veezy

---

# Environmental Identity

Give the dungeon a strong visual identity.

Examples:

* floating rooms connected by chains
* endless library corridors
* volcanic caverns
* rooms filled with water

---

# Guardians

Guardians should match the theme.

Examples:

* constructs
* summoned outsiders
* magical traps
* cursed spirits

---

# Unique Mechanics

Each dungeon should contain a **distinct mechanic**.

Examples:

* rooms rearrange themselves
* gravity changes direction
* illusions distort reality
* magical contracts bind players

---

# Dungeon Twist

The dungeon should contain a narrative surprise.

Examples:

* the artifact is sentient
* the dungeon itself is alive
* removing the artifact triggers catastrophe
* the artifact is not what it appears

---

# Example Dungeon

Theme: Celestial Museum

Original Owner: Solar archivists

Reason: Preserve divine relics

Artifact: Halo of the First Dawn

Environment:

* marble halls
* floating display cases
* radiant light everywhere

Guardians:

* animated statues
* angelic constructs

Mechanic:

* relics test the morality of intruders

Twist:

* the halo removes free will from its wearer
