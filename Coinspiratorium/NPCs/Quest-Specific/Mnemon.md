# Mnemon

The Memory Sheriff. An advanced aboleth guarding the Archive of the First Knowing. Ancient even by aboleth standards, treats unauthorized memory access the way a copyright lawyer treats piracy. Encountered in Quest 06.

## Dungeon Context

Mnemon resides beyond the Deep Archive (Room 7) in a deeper corridor inaccessible to PCs. When the pearl alarm triggers in Room 9, Mnemon arrives 3 minutes later from this corridor — entering the Pedestal Chamber through a sealed passage behind the pedestal alcove.

### Savant-Violation Modifier

If the PCs explored Room 8 (the Aboleth Savant's Cell) before triggering the alarm, Mnemon is aware of the intrusion. The Savant is one of Mnemon's catalogued assets — a living search engine under Mnemon's direct jurisdiction. Unauthorized interaction with the Savant constitutes an additional violation:

> *YOU HAVE ALSO ACCESSED RESTRICTED COGNITIVE ASSET 7-THETA WITHOUT AUTHORIZATION. THE SAVANT IS NOT A PUBLIC TERMINAL. THIS CONSTITUTES A CLASS-4 VIOLATION IN ADDITION TO YOUR CLASS-7 REMOVAL OF CORE ASSET 001.*

**Mechanical effect:** All negotiation DCs with Mnemon increase by +2 if the PCs interacted with the Savant (whether through combat or the query interface). If the PCs *killed* the Savant, Mnemon's opening disposition shifts from "bureaucratic" to "hostile" — it skips the negotiation phase entirely and attacks, treating the destruction of a catalogued asset as an irreversible offense.

### Combat Entrance

Mnemon enters Room 9 from a sealed passage that opens in the wall behind the pedestal. The passage glows with deep bioluminescence as Mnemon approaches — PCs get 1 round of warning (the light, the temperature drop, the shift in the vault's ambient hum) before Mnemon emerges.
