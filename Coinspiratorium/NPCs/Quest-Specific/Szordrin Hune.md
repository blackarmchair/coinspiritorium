**Szordrin Hune** is a male drow in his middle years (by elven standards — he looks 30, he's 200). Lean, sharp-featured, with white hair cropped short and a thin scar across his throat from a punishment he doesn't discuss. He dresses in practical dark leathers, not the ornate robes of the priestess caste, and carries himself like a man who's been looking over his shoulder for so long it's become posture.
