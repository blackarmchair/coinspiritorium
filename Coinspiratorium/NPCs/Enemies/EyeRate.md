## EyeRate
*Collections / Enforcement*

EyeRate is impulsive, volatile, and genuinely unstable in a way that makes even other Eyecons nervous. He doesn't understand moderation — only escalation — and treats debt collection as a deeply personal, almost spiritual experience.

Veezy keeps him on a leash (sometimes literally) because EyeRate will happily annihilate assets he's supposed to recover. When EyeRate is deployed, negotiations are already over.

**Vibe:** Attack dog who thinks interest is a moral concept.

**Role at the table:** The threat. When the PCs fall behind on tribute, EyeRate shows up. His presence alone should raise the stakes. He's not there to talk — he's there because talking failed.

---

## Appearance
- Stocky and dense for a beholder-kin — 3.5 feet in diameter, covered in thick, scarred hide the color of a bruise
- Six eyestalks that twitch and jerk erratically, never quite pointing in the same direction
- Central eye is bloodshot, veined with gold (literally — Veezy had gold filaments threaded into the sclera as a "loyalty bonus")
- Several eyestalks have bite marks, burn scars, or crudely healed wounds — most self-inflicted during rages
- Mouth is disproportionately large, full of jagged teeth stained with something that might be blood, might be hot sauce
- Radiates heat. Literally. The air shimmers around him when he's angry (which is always)

### Eye Rays (Su)
Six eyestalks, up to **three rays per round**, targeting different creatures within **100 ft.** Save DC: **19** (Charisma-based).

| Eye Ray | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Repossession Beam** | *Disintegrate* — "the asset has been liquidated" |
| **Interest Accrual** | *Scorching Ray* (3 rays, 4d6 fire each) — each ray leaves a gold brand on impact |
| **Compound Fracture** | *Shatter* (heightened, DC 19) — targets equipment first, then bones |
| **Collection Notice** | *Fear* — EyeRate screams the target's outstanding balance while firing |
| **Late Fee** | *Inflict Serious Wounds* (3d8+12) — melee touch delivered via eyestalk |
| **Final Demand** | *Flesh to Stone* — target is petrified and stamped "ACCOUNT CLOSED" |

### Special Abilities

**Antimagic Cone (Su)**
Central eye emits a 100-foot cone of antimagic, as *antimagic field* (CL 12th). EyeRate rarely uses this — he'd rather shoot.

**Debt Collector's Maw (Ex)**
EyeRate's bite deals 2d6+7 damage with an improved critical range of 19-20. On a confirmed critical hit, the target must make a DC 21 Fort save or lose a worn magic item (EyeRate bites it off and swallows it — "repossessed").

**Volatile Rage (Ex)**
When EyeRate drops below half HP (57 hp), he enters a rage: +4 Str, +4 Con, -2 AC, and he can fire **four** eye rays per round instead of three. This lasts until combat ends or he's incapacitated. He cannot voluntarily end the rage.

**Overdue (Su)** — *1/day*
EyeRate designates one creature he can see and roars its debts into existence. The target must make a Will save (DC 19) or be **marked for collection** for 1 minute: EyeRate gains +2 to hit and +2d6 damage on all attacks against that creature, and the creature cannot become invisible or teleport.

**All-Around Vision (Ex)**
Cannot be flanked.

## Notes
- EyeRate is the most dangerous Eyecon in a straight fight — and the least dangerous in every other way
- He has no subtlety, no patience, and no indoor voice
- Veezy keeps him chained in a gilded kennel between deployments. EyeRate does not seem to mind this. He likes the chain. It's gold.
- The other Eyecons avoid him. Iris Major manages him. Snitch Ray documents his "incidents." EyeQ finds him fascinating in the way entomologists find wasps fascinating.
- In combat, EyeRate charges the nearest debtor and does not stop until something is broken
