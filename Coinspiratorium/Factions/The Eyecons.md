# The Eyecons
*Veezy's elite entourage.*

> Floating enablers, informants, illusionists, and managers.

---

## Overview
The Eyecons are [[Vizzok the Opulent|Lord Veezy's]] inner circle — a retinue of five advanced beholder-kin who serve as his managers, bodyguards, hype crew, and enforcers. Where Veezy goes, the Eyecons follow. They are the visible face of his operation when he's not present, and his amplifiers when he is.

They are the PCs' **direct handlers** — the faces they see most often, the voices that deliver contracts, the eyes that watch their every move.

## Notes
- Each Eyecon should feel like a distinct NPC the players have opinions about
- Some may be more sympathetic than others — EyeQ especially has potential as a turncoat or reluctant informant
- They're a great tool for delivering Veezy's will without Veezy being present
- Consider which Eyecon the PCs interact with most — that becomes the face of their daily oppression
- When the Crown collapses in the endgame, the Eyecons collapse into gold dust — make the players feel that
