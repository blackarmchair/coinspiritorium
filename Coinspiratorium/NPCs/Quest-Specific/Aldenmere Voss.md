# Aldenmere Voss
*Custodian. Creditor. Casualty.*

> *"Debt is not violence. Debt is memory. I simply ensure the memory is accurate."*

---

## Overview
Aldenmere Voss was the most respected debt-holder in [[Offledger]] — a human man who held power not through intimidation or flamboyance but through precision, principle, and an impeccable reputation for fair dealing. He served as custodian of [[The Instrument of Contested Debt]] for thirty years, a role he treated with the solemnity of a priesthood.

He is murdered during his annual gathering, the Recession, by [[Pettifer Groan]] — but the conditions that made his death inevitable were engineered by [[Vizzok the Opulent|Veezy]].

## Appearance
- Late sixties, thin as a contract's margin
- Silver hair pulled back tight
- Hands that have never held a weapon
- Dark formal clothes, no ornamentation except a single plain iron ring
- Eyes that have spent fifty years reading fine print and finding the clause that matters

## Personality
- **Precise.** Every word measured. Every gesture intentional.
- **Principled.** Deals fairly within the rules. The rules are not always fair.
- **Quietly powerful.** Never raises his voice. Doesn't need to.
- **Courteous.** Remembers names, children, old favors. Genuine warmth beneath the formality.
- **Aware.** In his final hours, he realizes the room is wrong — too many enemies, too many fresh wounds — but proceeds anyway because tradition demands it.

## Role
The murder victim in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. His death should matter. He is not a villain, not a fool, not a pawn. He is a good man destroyed by a better strategist.

## Stats
- **Race:** Human
- **Class/Level:** Expert 12
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **Notable Abilities:** Diplomacy +22, Sense Motive +20, Knowledge (Local) +18, Profession (Debt-Holder) +24

## Connections
- **Faction:** Independent — aligned with no criminal organization, which made him trusted and vulnerable
- **Allies:** Few; he was respected, not loved
- **Enemies:** [[Meralda Thorne]] (betrayed partner), [[Harath Kull]] (ruined debtor), [[Creditor Zhaal]] (rival claimant), [[Tesslyn Voss]] (impatient heir)
- **Relationship to Veezy:** Professional acquaintance. Voss considered Veezy *"energetic but ultimately conventional."* He was wrong.

## Notes
- Voss should be someone the PCs like or respect before he dies. Give him a moment of genuine warmth during the cocktail hour
- His iron ring is the opposite of Veezy's golden excess — plain, functional, meaningful. Two philosophies of power
- The note in his amended will — *"Tell her in person. She deserves that."* — reveals a man who cared about doing things right. He never got the chance
