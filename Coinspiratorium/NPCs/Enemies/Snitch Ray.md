## Snitch Ray
*Surveillance / Informants*

Snitch Ray is a bitter, joyless professional whose entire personality is "documenting your failure." He is constantly watching, constantly reporting, and constantly framing events in the least charitable way possible.

Even Veezy finds him annoying, but keeps him around because Snitch Ray is **always right** — and always loyal to power, not people. The PCs should feel like he's narcing on them even when they do nothing wrong.

**Vibe:** Internal affairs investigator who hates his own side more than the enemy.

**Role at the table:** Paranoia fuel. Every time the PCs do something shady, Snitch Ray saw it. Every time they cut a corner, Snitch Ray documented it. He's the reason they can't relax.

---

## Appearance
- Smaller than a standard beholder — roughly 3 feet in diameter, matte gray-green hide
- Six eyestalks, all unnaturally still, like surveillance cameras locked on targets
- Central eye is pale and unblinking, pupil contracted to a pinpoint
- No jewelry, no ornamentation — aggressively plain, as if accessories would compromise objectivity
- Carries a floating leather-bound ledger that writes itself when he speaks
- Faint smell of ink and cold stone

### Eye Rays (Su)
Each of Snitch Ray's six eyestalks produces a ray once per round as a free action. He can use up to **two rays per round**, targeting different creatures within **120 ft.** Save DC: **19** (Wisdom-based).

| Eye Ray | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Scrutiny Beam** | *Detect Thoughts* (continuous, no save; target unaware) |
| **Snitch Bolt** | *Hold Person* — target is paralyzed while Snitch Ray "documents the incident" |
| **Exposure Ray** | *Glitterdust* — reveals invisible creatures; -40 to Hide for 1d4+1 rounds |
| **Dossier Beam** | *Zone of Truth* (15 ft. radius centered on target) |
| **Whistleblower Pulse** | *Sending* — instantly transmits a 25-word report to [[Vizzok the Opulent\|Veezy]] or any Eyecon |
| **Blackout Ray** | *Silence* (15 ft. radius, 6 rounds) — "This conversation is being recorded" |

---

### Special Abilities

**Antimagic Cone (Su)**
Central eye emits a 100-foot cone of antimagic, as *antimagic field* (CL 11th). All eye rays are suppressed within the cone.

**Total Recall (Ex)**
Snitch Ray has perfect eidetic memory. He can recall any conversation, transaction, or event he has witnessed with complete accuracy. Anything said or done in his presence is permanently on the record.

**Paranoia Aura (Su)**
All creatures within 30 ft. must make a Will save (DC 17) or become **uneasy**: -2 to Bluff checks and Sleight of Hand checks, and the creature feels watched even after leaving the area (1 hour duration).

**All-Around Vision (Ex)**
Cannot be flanked.

## Notes
- Snitch Ray is not a combat threat — he's an information threat. His danger is what he *reports*, not what he *does*
- In combat, he retreats immediately and documents from a distance
- His floating ledger is a minor artifact: fireproof, waterproof, and magically linked to a master copy in Veezy's vault
- The worst thing a PC can hear: *"Noted."*
