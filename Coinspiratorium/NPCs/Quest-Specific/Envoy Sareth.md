# Envoy Sareth
*The one who figured it out too late.*

> *"Your employer didn't just find this opportunity. He built it. And you're the collection mechanism."*

---

## Overview
An [[O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E.]] deep-cover agent who has been tracking [[Vizzok the Opulent|Veezy]]'s financial movements for months. She attends the Recession disguised as a minor debt-holder from a distant plane. She expected a financial maneuver. She got a murder. She's improvising.

## Appearance
- Mid-thirties, half-elf, unremarkable by design
- Brown hair, simple clothes, nothing memorable
- Carries herself like someone who has practiced being forgettable
- When she drops her cover, her posture changes — sharper, more alert, recognizably trained

## Personality
- **Professional.** Calm under pressure. Analytical.
- **Adaptable.** Expected one situation, got another, pivoted without panic.
- **Genuinely concerned.** Unlike some O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. agents, she's not just following orders. She understands what the [[The Device|Crown Infinite]] means and she's scared.
- **Respectful of the PCs.** She's read their file. She knows what they've done. She doesn't underestimate them.

## Role
The O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. complication in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. Not a suspect — a variable. She reveals herself mid-investigation and attempts to seize the Instrument or prevent the claim. She fails (Offledger's sovereignty trumps her warrant), but her presence escalates the O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. thread.

## Stats
- **Race:** Half-elf
- **Class/Level:** Rogue 8 / Expert 4

- **Notable Abilities:** Disguise +18, Sense Motive +16, Bluff +15, Knowledge (Planar) +12, Sneak Attack +4d6

## Connections
- **Faction:** [[O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E.]]
- **Reports to:** [[Agent Quill]] (handler)
- **Allies:** The agency; she's a team player
- **Enemies:** Veezy's operation, by mandate
- **Relationship to Veezy:** She's been tracking him. She respects his intelligence and fears his ambition.

## Notes
- If the PCs have previously interacted with [[Agent Quill]] or [[Auditor Pentival]], Sareth uses similar verbal tics — bureaucratic precision, casual competence. A very high Sense Motive (DC 25) catches this during the cocktail hour
- She's the one who delivers the line: *"He built it. And you're the collection mechanism."* — make this land
- After the quest, she becomes a potential recurring contact. She's less manipulative than Quill, more field-practical than Pentival. She might actually like the PCs
