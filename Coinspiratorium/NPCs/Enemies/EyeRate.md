## EyeRate
*Collections / Enforcement*

EyeRate is impulsive, volatile, and genuinely unstable in a way that makes even other Eyecons nervous. He doesn't understand moderation — only escalation — and treats debt collection as a deeply personal, almost spiritual experience.

Veezy keeps him on a leash (sometimes literally) because EyeRate will happily annihilate assets he's supposed to recover. When EyeRate is deployed, negotiations are already over.

**Vibe:** Attack dog who thinks interest is a moral concept.

**Role at the table:** The threat. When the PCs fall behind on tribute, EyeRate shows up. His presence alone should raise the stakes. He's not there to talk — he's there because talking failed.
