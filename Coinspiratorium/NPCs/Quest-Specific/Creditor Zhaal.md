# Creditor Zhaal
*The patient claimant.*

> *"I have waited twenty years for the law to deliver what is mine. I will not accept a thief's shortcut."*

---

## Overview
A githyanki debt-knight whose claim on [[The Instrument of Contested Debt]] predates [[Aldenmere Voss]]'s custodianship — but not Veezy's excavated claim. Has attended every Recession for twenty years, waiting for a legal opportunity. Honorable, rigid, and carrying a silver sword.

## Appearance
- Tall, gaunt, angular features typical of githyanki
- Full plate armor with debt-court insignia
- Silver sword at his side — always
- Moves like someone who has been waiting a long time and has no intention of waiting any longer

## Personality
- **Honor-bound.** Fights fair, speaks true, follows rules
- **Frustrated.** Twenty years of patience, and a beholder crime lord's paperwork beats him
- **Respects strength.** If defeated in debt-combat, accepts the result with dignity

## Role
Competing claimant and potential combat encounter in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. His alibi is airtight (in the main room the whole time). He challenges the PCs' claim through debt-combat.

## Stats
- **Race:** Githyanki
- **Class/Level:** Fighter 12
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **HP:** 108
- **AC:** 24 (full plate + Dex + ring of protection)
- **Attack:** +1 Silver Sword +18/+13/+8 melee (2d6+7, 17-20/×2, severs astral cord on crit)
- **Key Feats:** Power Attack, Cleave, Great Cleave, Improved Critical (greatsword), Combat Expertise
- **Tactics:** Honorable. Fights to first blood (50% HP) unless dishonored.

## Connections
- **Faction:** Githyanki Debt-Courts
- **Relationship to Voss:** Respected rival claimant
- **Relationship to Veezy:** Contempt — considers Veezy's methods dishonorable
- **Relationship to PCs:** Potential enemy or respect-rival depending on how the claim is handled

## Notes
- If defeated honorably, Zhaal owes the PCs a debt-favor — which is holy currency to a githyanki
- If the PCs refuse debt-combat, Zhaal's claim is dismissed but he becomes a permanent enemy
- He leaves Offledger after the quest regardless of outcome
