# Offledger
*Sovereign extradimensional microstate of dubious legality.*

---

## Overview
Offledger is the **campaign hub** — an illegally-created demiplane that serves as [[Vizzok the Opulent|Lord Veezy's]] sovereign territory and the setting's equivalent of **Mos Eisley**. It's a haven for criminals, outcasts, antisocial elements, and all kinds of esoteric and misanthropic wanderers. Everything here operates outside the jurisdiction of any planar authority — at least, that's what Veezy claims.

[[The Coinspiratorium (Location)|The Coinspiratorium]] is located here, along with whatever other establishments, markets, and dens the campaign needs.

## Description
*Think Mos Eisley cantina meets extradimensional black market:*
- A sprawling, chaotic settlement floating in its own pocket dimension
- No consistent architecture — buildings from a dozen planes crammed together
- Neon-bright magical signage clashing with crumbling stone facades
- Streets that don't always go where they went yesterday
- Gravity is mostly a suggestion; some districts are sideways or inverted
- The "sky" is a swirling void of gold-tinged nothing
- Always bustling: traders, thieves, exiles, fugitives, and creatures that don't fit anywhere else
- Smells like incense, ozone, and bad decisions

## The Population
Offledger attracts:
- **Criminals** — black market dealers, smugglers, fences
- **Outcasts** — beings exiled from their home planes
- **Antisocial elements** — people who don't play well with organized society
- **Esoteric wanderers** — planar travelers, researchers, collectors of forbidden knowledge
- **Misanthropic drifters** — people who came here to disappear
- **Entrepreneurs of dubious morality** — shop owners, service providers, and hustlers

Everyone here has a reason to be off the grid. Nobody asks too many questions.

## Notable Locations
- **[[The Coinspiratorium (Location)]]** — the PCs' shop and home base
- **Veezy's Palace** — his private sanctum within Offledger (restricted access)
- **The Workshop** — where [[The Device|the P.L.O.T. Device / Crown Infinite]] is being assembled *(PCs may not know this exists initially)*
- **The Portal Hub** — connections to the material plane and other operations
- *Other establishments TBD — taverns, rival shops, black markets, fighting pits, etc.*

## The Device: Environmental Changes
As the campaign progresses, the PCs should notice changes to Offledger itself:
- Arcane circuits lighting up in walls and streets
- Strange vibrations through the ground
- Gravity anomalies — things floating, falling sideways
- New structures appearing that weren't there before
- Components the PCs recovered from dungeons being installed into the architecture
- The "sky" flickering, pulsing, or showing brief images

This is [[The Device|the Crown Infinite]] taking shape. Offledger isn't just Veezy's territory — it may be part of the device itself.

## Notes
- Offledger is the social sandbox — the PCs can explore, shop, make contacts, get into trouble, and encounter plot hooks just by walking around
- The Mos Eisley vibe means every NPC has a story and every corner has a hook
- It should feel lived-in, dangerous, and weirdly charming — a place the PCs might actually like despite everything
- The slow environmental changes as the Crown takes shape should be unsettling — the place they call home is becoming something else
- Consider developing distinct districts or neighborhoods with different vibes
