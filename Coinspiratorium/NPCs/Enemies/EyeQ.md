## EyeQ
*Architect / Thinker*

EyeQ is quiet, analytical, and unsettlingly detached, treating people and planes as interchangeable components in a system. He is the **mind behind [[The Device|the Crown Infinite's]] architecture**, genuinely fascinated by debt as a universal constant rather than a moral problem.

Unlike the others, EyeQ doesn't worship Veezy — he **studies** him, as a uniquely useful anomaly. If anyone understands how the Crown can fail, it's EyeQ... and that knowledge makes him extremely dangerous.

**Vibe:** Brilliant engineer who already lives in the post-collapse world.

**Role at the table:** The wildcard. He's the Eyecon most likely to become an unexpected ally, an unexpected betrayer, or the one who drops a cryptic hint that changes everything. He knows more than he says. Always.

---

## Appearance
- The smallest Eyecon — barely 2.5 feet in diameter, dark matte hide with faint geometric patterns that shift when you're not looking directly at them
- Six eyestalks that move independently with eerie precision, like instruments taking measurements
- Central eye is pale silver, almost white — looks through things rather than at them
- No adornments except a series of thin arcane sigils etched directly into his hide, glowing faintly blue
- Hovers perfectly still for long periods, then moves in sudden, precise trajectories
- Speaks rarely, quietly, and always as if continuing a conversation he's been having with himself

## Connections
- **Faction:** [[The Gold Standard]] — [[The Eyecons|Eyecon]], Arcane Architecture Division
- **Reports to:** [[Vizzok the Opulent]] (nominally)
- **Key relationship:** Designed and built [[The Device|the Crown Infinite's]] architecture
- **Relationship to PCs:** The one who knows what the artifacts are really for. The one who might tell them — if it serves his calculations.

### Eye Rays (Su)
Six eyestalks, up to **three rays per round**, targeting different creatures within **150 ft.** Save DC: **20** (Charisma-based, or **23** Intelligence-based for Schematic Ray).

| Eye Ray | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Schematic Ray** | *Maze* — target is trapped in a pocket dimension shaped like a recursive architectural blueprint. Int DC 23 to escape |
| **Deconstruction Beam** | *Disintegrate* — EyeQ disassembles things at a molecular level, clinically |
| **Analysis Pulse** | *Greater Dispel Magic* (targeted) — EyeQ unravels magical effects by understanding their structure |
| **Blueprint Ray** | *Dimensional Anchor* — target cannot teleport or shift planes; a faint grid pattern appears on their skin |
| **Hypothesis Beam** | *Slow* — "reducing variables for observation" |
| **Contingency Bolt** | *Contingent ray*: EyeQ pre-programs one ray to fire automatically when a specified condition is met (set during his turn, triggers as an immediate action) |

---

### Special Abilities

**Antimagic Cone (Su)**
Central eye emits a 130-foot cone of antimagic, as *antimagic field* (CL 12th). EyeQ uses this surgically — isolating specific targets rather than suppressing whole areas.

**Architect's Sight (Su)**
EyeQ can see magical auras, planar boundaries, structural stress points, and invisible creatures within 120 ft. at all times (functions as permanent *arcane sight*, *see invisibility*, and *true seeing* simultaneously).

**Recursive Thinking (Ex)**
EyeQ can take two standard actions per round instead of one, but only if both are used for eye rays or spell-like abilities (not movement or physical attacks). This represents his ability to process multiple calculations simultaneously.

**Crown Infinite Resonance (Su)**
EyeQ is attuned to [[The Device|the Crown Infinite]]. He can sense when a Crown component is within 1 mile, identify its function, and determine its current state. This is how Veezy finds the artifacts — through EyeQ's calculations.

**All-Around Vision (Ex)**
Cannot be flanked.

## Notes
- EyeQ is not loyal to Veezy. He is loyal to the *project*. The Crown Infinite is the most interesting thing he has ever encountered. Veezy is merely the funding source.
- If the PCs demonstrate they understand the Crown's architecture, EyeQ will become intensely interested in them — possibly to recruit them, possibly to dissect them, possibly both
- He speaks in fragments and half-finished thoughts: *"The third component resonates at... no. Irrelevant. You wouldn't... hmm. Actually. What do you know about recursive debt?"*
- He is the only Eyecon who could plausibly betray Veezy — not out of morality, but because the math might demand it
- His Mordenkainen's Disjunction is a last resort. He has used it once. The crater is still there.
- **Contrast with Auditor Pentival:** Both understand the Crown. EyeQ built it. Pentival can break it. They have never met. That meeting would be either the most productive or the most catastrophic conversation in the multiverse.
