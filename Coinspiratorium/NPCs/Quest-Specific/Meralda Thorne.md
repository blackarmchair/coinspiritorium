# Meralda Thorne
*The betrayed partner.*

> *"I wanted him to suffer. I didn't want him to die. There's a distance between those two things and I have lived in it for forty years."*

---

## Overview
Co-founder of Voss's debt-holding firm, bought out forty years ago through a clause she didn't read. She has attended every Recession since — part vigil, part accusation, part inability to let go. She didn't kill Voss. She wanted to. That's the worst part.

## Appearance
- Half-elf woman in her fifties, impeccably dressed in dark green
- Still carries the ceremonial dagger from their partnership dissolution
- Drinks steadily but never loses composure

## Personality
- Bitter, dignified, honest about her bitterness
- If cleared of suspicion, she's grateful — and gives the PCs her dagger as thanks

## Role
Red herring / emotional witness in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. Strong motive, strong presence, innocent.

## Connections
- **Relationship to Voss:** Former partner, forty-year grudge
- **Relationship to Veezy:** Veezy's network leaked the fraudulent buyout documents to inflame her resentment. She doesn't know this

## Notes
- Her dagger is a +1 ceremonial weapon worth 800gp. If given to the PCs, it's a token of genuine gratitude from a woman who doesn't give those easily
