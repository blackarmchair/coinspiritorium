# Harath Kull
*The ruined debtor.*

> *"I've been carrying a knife for two years. It's still clean."*

---

## Overview
A dwarf once wealthy, now hollowed out. [[Aldenmere Voss]] called in his debts two years ago and took everything — foundry, contracts, reputation. Harath lives in Offledger's lower districts now. He shouldn't be at the Recession. His invitation arrived unexpectedly. Someone paid off his remaining minor debts to make him eligible to attend.

That someone was [[Vizzok the Opulent|Veezy]]'s network.

## Appearance
- Dwarf, middle-aged, wearing clothes that used to fit better
- Hollow-eyed, calloused hands
- Carries a plain knife. Has never used it.

## Personality
- **Broken.** Not angry — *empty.* Voss took everything and left him with the shape of a life.
- **Honest under pressure.** If pushed hard enough, admits he thought about killing Voss but couldn't go through with it.
- **Sympathetic.** The PCs should feel for him. He's the human cost of Offledger's economy.

## Role
Sympathetic red herring in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. No alibi for the critical window (was near the servants' passage). Didn't do it. His breakdown under questioning is one of the investigation's most human moments.

## Connections
- **Relationship to Voss:** Voss destroyed him. Legally. Completely.
- **Relationship to Veezy:** Veezy's network paid off his minor debts to make his attendance possible. Harath doesn't know this. He thought it was a clerical error in his favor.

## Notes
- Harath is Veezy's system made flesh. He was ruined by debt law. He was placed at the gathering by financial manipulation. He exists in this story because someone decided he was useful as a suspect. He's a prop in someone else's plan, and he doesn't even know it
