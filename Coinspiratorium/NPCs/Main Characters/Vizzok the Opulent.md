# Vizzok the Opulent
*AKA Lord Veezy*

*Beholder. Mogul. Visionary. Problem.*

> *"You don't owe me money. You owe me everything. The money is just how we keep score."*

---

## Overview
Vizzok the Opulent, better known by his street name **Lord Veezy**, is a beholder of extraordinary wealth, outlandish self-regard, and unpredictable influence. Once a low-level enforcer for a planar crime ring, Vizzok clawed his way up the multiversal black market through sheer charisma, arcane branding, and the occasional disintegration.

Now, he runs [[The Coinspiratorium (Location)|the Coinspiratorium]], a gleaming criminal haven floating in its own pocket plane — part marketplace, part front, part metaphysical flex. He considers it not merely a business, but a *"platform for transformative commerce-based enlightenment."*

**Home Plane:** Excommunicated from the material plane
**Current Residence:** [[Offledger]], sovereign extradimensional microstate of dubious legality

---

## Tone & DM Guidance
Veezy is the **BBEG**, but he's also one of the most entertaining characters at the table. He's **Kanye West as a D&D villain** — a gangsta-rapper beholder crime lord in a fantasy setting.

**The Mix:**
- **Humorous:** Grandiose rants, absurd self-aggrandizement, eye puns, bling obsession, treating dungeon contracts like record deals
- **Intimidating:** Sudden tonal shifts to genuine menace, casual disintegrations, the raw alien horror of a beholder when the mask slips
- **The key:** The comedy makes the threat *worse*. When he's funny, the players relax. When he stops being funny, they know someone's about to die.

**Voice Notes:**
- Speaks like a prophet, acts like a tycoon. Sentences sound like riddles, TED Talks, or marketing slogans. All of them end in profit.
- Delivers eye puns with complete sincerity ("I've got my eye on you — all of them." / "Vision is everything. I would know.")
- Grandiose rants about his brand, his legacy, his *aesthetic*
- Over-the-top violence delivered casually ("Disintegrate him. No wait — gild him first. I need a new coat rack.")

**Sample Quotes:**
- *"I didn't build an empire. I manifested a lifestyle."*
- *"You think you're broke? Broke is a mindset. You're in debt. Debt is a relationship. And baby, we are EXCLUSIVE."*
- *"Every eye I have sees profit. You know what I see when I look at you? Overhead."*
- *"I'm not a criminal. I'm an unlicensed visionary operating outside the jurisdiction of lesser minds."*
- *"You brought me copper? COPPER? Do I look like a wishing well to you?"*

## Appearance
- A **Huge** beholder draped in gold — chains, rings, and enchanted accessories on nearly every eye stalk
- Every eyestalk wears **custom-forged rings**
- His central eye is often fitted with an **enchanted monocle engraved with his own face**
- Wears **bespoke enchanted grills** on his central maw (the Diamond Grill — see combat)
- Floats in a perpetual shimmer of residual enchantment magic and expensive incense
- His central eye is unsettlingly golden, as if the iris itself were minted
- Never seen without at least one eye stalk wearing a **solid gold signet ring the size of a teacup**

## Personality
- **Flamboyant.** Veezy doesn't enter a room — he *arrives*.
- **Terrifyingly charming.** Despite being an aberration, he has fans. And brand ambassadors.
- **Fashion-obsessed.** Treats his accessories as extensions of his identity. Insulting his jewelry is a death sentence.
- **Magnanimous (when paid).** Generous with praise and rewards when tribute flows on schedule.
- **Lethal (when stiffed).** Shifts instantly from charm to vaporization over a missing copper piece.
- **Collector.** Obsessed with magical curios, rare items, and unique treasures.
- **Delusional visionary.** Genuinely believes he's improving the multiverse through "disruptive commerce."

## Role in the Campaign
Veezy is the **BBEG** — but he's also the boss who *signs your paycheck*. The players work for him, owe him, and must navigate his demands while trying to earn their freedom.

**What he tells the PCs:** Run the shop. Clear the dungeons. Pay the debt.
**What he's actually doing:** Collecting 12 specific artifacts to build [[The Device|the Crown Infinite]] — a reality-modifying arcane megastructure that will make him a god whose domain is "clout." The PCs are his unwitting procurement team.

**His fatal flaw:** He assumes the PCs can never repay their debt. He's wrong. See [[Veezy's IOU]].

### What He Wants (Overtly)
1. Regular tribute payments — on time, in full
2. The Coinspiratorium to turn a profit
3. Rare and unusual items — he takes a personal cut of anything "interesting"
4. Respect (groveling is preferred but not strictly required — it is strictly required)
5. Expansion of his empire

### What He Wants (Secretly)
- 12 specific artifacts that form the circuit of [[The Device|the Crown Infinite]]
- These components are being installed in [[Offledger]] — arcane circuits, strange devices, reality-warping tech
- He's building a device that rewrites reality's laws: influence becomes power, debt becomes destiny, gold becomes identity
- He wants to become a **fixed multiversal constant** — a god whose domain is relevance
- The PCs' own debt contract is one of the four keystones (see [[The Device#Component I The Ledger Foundation (Binding Souls)]])

## Enemies
- **[[O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E.]]** — a planar extradition force from his home dimension, seeking to bring him to justice. He calls them *"jealous former associates with a fear of evolution."*
- Various deities of order, commerce, and restraint, who find his blend of greed and glamour cosmically offensive
- Rival crime lords and anyone who's ever shorted him a copper

---

## How to Use Him
- He's the quest-giver who can't be refused
- He's the tension ratchet — raise his demands when things get too comfortable
- He's the ticking clock — debt accrues, and he notices
- He's also, occasionally, a lifeline — he protects his investments
- He's the slow-burn reveal — the "fetch quests" are building toward something terrible
- **The comedy is the weapon.** Players laugh, players relax, players stop asking why he wants that specific artifact. Then it's too late.

### Special Abilities

**Antimagic Cone (Su)**
The central eye emits a 150-foot cone of antimagic, as *antimagic field* (CL 16th). All eye rays except the cone are suppressed within it.

**Diamond Grill Chomp (Ex)**
Bite attack deals 2d6+6 plus 1d6 fire and 1d6 sonic. On hit, target must make a DC 20 Fort save or be dazzled for 1 round.

**Burning Lean (Su)** — *1/day*
Veezy splashes a 20-ft cone of intoxicating purple fire from a platinum goblet.
- 5d6 fire damage (Reflex DC 22 half)
- Failed Will save (DC 20) = *confusion* for 1d4 rounds
- If a creature dies while confused, it becomes a golden statue mid-swagger

**Presence of Opulence (Ex)**
All creatures within 30 ft. must make a Will save (DC 20) or be **bedazzled:**
- -2 to attacks and Will saves vs. charm/fear
- Cannot target his jewelry
- On 3 failed saves, target is *fascinated* for 1 round

**All-Around Vision (Ex)**
Beholders see in all directions. They cannot be flanked.

## Notes
- Lord Veezy is the platinum-plated Don of Offledger, a master of eye magic and economic ruin. His charm is lethal, his grill legendary, and his goblet always full of something purple and flammable.
- Players should fear him but also find him *hilarious*.
- His power level should always feel just beyond what the players can handle — until maybe, someday, it isn't.
- The slow reveal that the "random" artifacts he's been requesting are components of something catastrophic is the campaign's central dramatic turn.
