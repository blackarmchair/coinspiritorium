## Major Look
*Image / Discipline / Public Order*

Major Look is obsessed with appearances, optics, and "how things read." He believes disorder is weakness, embarrassment is failure, and anything sloppy reflects poorly on the brand. He handles punishments meant to be **seen**, not necessarily to kill — public examples, humiliations, disappearances with messaging.

Major Look genuinely thinks he's the good guy, because in his mind, chaos is the real villain.

**Vibe:** Authoritarian PR guy who thinks he's saving everyone from themselves.

**Role at the table:** He's the one who shows up when the PCs embarrass the brand. Sold a cursed item to the wrong customer? Major Look wants a word. Got into a public brawl? Major Look is *concerned about optics*. He makes the PCs feel like employees at a toxic company — which they are.
