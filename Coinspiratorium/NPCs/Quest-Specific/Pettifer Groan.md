# Pettifer Groan
*The one who pulled the trigger.*

> **DM EYES ONLY** — Pettifer is the killer in Quest 11.

---

## Overview
An information broker who works with everyone in [[Offledger]] — including Voss, including Veezy, including [[O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E.]]. Pettifer killed [[Aldenmere Voss]] with a poisoned needle because he was told — through Veezy's network, falsely — that Voss planned to release compromising documents about him at the Recession.

He acted out of fear, not malice. He is barely holding together.

## Appearance
- Gnome, middle-aged, impeccably groomed
- Talks constantly — a professional habit that serves as both shield and tell
- Hands shake when he's not performing

## Personality
- **Professional liar.** His cover story is rehearsed and solid. But his nerve is fraying.
- **Frightened.** He killed a man to protect himself from a threat that didn't exist. He doesn't know it was a lie.
- **Not a monster.** He's a frightened person who did a terrible thing because someone made him believe it was necessary.

## Role
The killer in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. His guilt is discoverable through investigation. Within the Instrument's adjudication field, his lies about financial motives cause his voice to fade.

## Evidence
- Alibi inconsistencies (describes wine and books in wrong rooms)
- Threader's Bane residue on the study door handle
- Voice fades in adjudication field when claiming no financial motive
- Knowledge (Nature) or Craft (Alchemy) DC 20 identifies the gnome-crafted poison

## Connections
- **Relationship to Voss:** Client and blackmail target. Voss held compromising documents.
- **Relationship to Veezy:** Veezy's network fed Pettifer the false intelligence that Voss would release the documents. Pettifer doesn't know the tip was manufactured.
- **Relationship to PCs:** If they protect him, he owes them — and his information network is valuable currency in Offledger.

## Notes
- Pettifer is a tragic figure. He's the murder weapon that Veezy loaded and aimed without ever touching. Let the PCs feel the weight of that
- If arrested, he's removed from play. If protected, he becomes a useful (and deeply grateful) contact
- The PCs may eventually realize the false intelligence came from Veezy's network. This makes Pettifer not just the killer but another victim of the same system
