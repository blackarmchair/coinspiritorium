## Director Casseel
*Director of the Vizzok Task Force*

Casseel is a former deva — a celestial being who willingly surrendered her divine station to accept a position of planar legal authority. She did this centuries ago. She has regretted it every single day since, but she would do it again, because someone has to.

She is the most tired being in the multiverse. Not dramatically tired — not burnt-out in a way that's cool or tragic. Just... **profoundly, bureaucratically exhausted.** She has filed ten thousand reports. She has tracked a hundred Veezys. She has watched civilizations rise and fall and fill out the wrong forms. And she is still here. Still working. Because the work doesn't stop.

**Appearance:**
- Tall, gaunt, ageless. Traces of celestial beauty worn down by paperwork and centuries of disappointment
- Pale gray garb, perfectly pressed, never wrinkled, never stylish
- A faint residual glow where her halo used to be, visible only in dim light, like a tan line
- Eyes that have seen everything and are no longer impressed by any of it
- Carries a slim folio that she never opens because she's memorized every page

**Personality:**
- **Measured.** Never raises her voice. Never needs to. Her disappointment is louder than any shout.
- **Precise.** Says exactly what she means. No more. No less. Does not do small talk.
- **Weary but unbreakable.** She's tired of evil. Tired of schemes. Tired of flamboyant crime lords who think they're special. But she will never, ever stop pursuing them.
- **Genuinely moral.** Unlike the agents under her, Casseel actually cares about justice — not just results. This makes her both the most trustworthy and the most dangerous person in O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E., because she will burn her own operation before she lets the wrong thing happen.
- **Has no patience for Veezy's theatrics.** She finds him exhausting in a way that's almost personal.

**Vibe:** The principal who's called you into the office. Your fourth-grade teacher who isn't angry, just disappointed. A saint who became a cop because heaven wasn't getting results.

**Sample Quotes:**
- *"I've been doing this for four hundred years. You are not the most interesting case on my desk. You are not even in the top fifty. But you are the one I'm looking at right now, and that should concern you."*
- *"I traded eternity for this badge. Don't waste my time."*
- *"Vizzok believes he's unprecedented. He isn't. He's just loud."*
- *"I'm offering you a way out. I won't offer twice."*
- *"I don't need you to be brave. I need you to be useful."*

**Role at the table:** Casseel is the moral weight. She makes the PCs feel the gravity of what they've been doing for Veezy. She doesn't guilt-trip — she just *sees clearly*, and that's worse. She's the only NPC in the campaign who might actually be trying to do the right thing, which makes her offer genuinely tempting and genuinely complicated.

**Contrast with Veezy:** Veezy is all spectacle, ego, and charm. Casseel is silence, duty, and resolve. He fills a room with gold; she fills it with the weight of consequences. Meeting her after a Veezy monologue should feel like a cold shower.
