## Auditor Pentival
*Forensic Analyst / Financial Crimes*

Auditor Pentival is a gnome. A very, very focused gnome. He is the foremost expert in metaphysical financial forensics in the multiverse, and he has followed Veezy's money through seventeen planes, nine shell companies, four temporal loops, and a pocket dimension that technically doesn't exist.

He is the most dangerous person in O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. — not because he can fight, but because he **understands debt as physics.** He sees the equations that hold reality together. He is the one who will accidentally reveal that the Crown Infinite has a vulnerability. And he's the one who can explain to the PCs what [[Veezy's IOU]] actually means.

**Appearance:**
- Small even for a gnome — barely three feet tall, easily overlooked (which he prefers)
- Thick spectacles that magnify his eyes to unsettling proportions
- Always carrying a battered leather case full of ledgers, notes, and charts covered in equations that hurt to look at
- Ink-stained fingers. Ink-stained cuffs. Ink stain on his nose that's been there for years.
- Talks with his hands, often while still holding a quill, leaving ink trails in the air

**Personality:**
- **Obsessive.** Once he latches onto a financial thread, he will follow it until it resolves or he dies. There is no middle setting.
- **Enthusiastic about numbers.** Genuinely lights up when discussing compound interest, debt obligations, or metaphysical accounting. This is alarming to everyone around him.
- **Socially oblivious.** Doesn't read rooms. Doesn't notice tension. Will launch into a lecture about recursive debt structures while people are pointing swords at each other.
- **Accidentally brilliant.** His most important revelations come out as offhand comments, tangents, or footnotes to completely unrelated observations.
- **Genuinely kind.** Unlike the other agents, Pentival has no agenda with the PCs beyond his work. He might actually help them if they ask the right questions — not because he's an ally, but because he can't resist explaining how an interesting number works.

**Vibe:** Your uncle who's a forensic accountant and won't shut up about it at Thanksgiving, except his work could topple a god.

**Sample Quotes:**
- *"Fascinating, fascinating — do you see? The debt structure here creates a recursive loop. If the Crown's primary creditor has any outstanding obligations of his own... well, the math gets rather exciting."*
- *"I don't mean to alarm anyone, but if Lord Vizzok ever possesses outstanding obligations — unpaid, binding, compounded — the Crown would destabilize instantly. Anyway, that's probably irrelevant. Where was I?"*
- *"I followed the gold. That's all. You'd be surprised how much evil is just... accounting errors."*
- *"Oh, you found a debt instrument? May I see it? ...Oh. Oh my. Oh, this is — do you understand what this is? This is a *singularity.*"*
- *"I'm not a field agent. I've told them this. They keep sending me into the field. I think there's been a clerical error."*

**Role at the table:** Pentival is the key to the endgame. He's the NPC who — through sheer enthusiasm for numbers — reveals that the Crown has a weakness. He's also comedic relief within O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E., the one member who doesn't fit the sleek, humorless aesthetic. He's a gnome with ink on his nose surrounded by fallen angels and constructs, and he's somehow the most important person in the room.

**The IOU Reveal:** When the PCs eventually show Pentival the IOU (or he stumbles across it in their records), his reaction should be the most dramatic thing a gnome has ever done. He will go pale. He will go silent. And then he will explain, in precise mathematical terms, exactly how a 49-gold-piece loan for a cape and a mixtape is about to collapse the fabric of reality.

**Contrast with EyeQ:** Both are the brains of their respective organizations. Both understand the Crown Infinite's architecture. But EyeQ is cold and detached — he sees the Crown as an intellectual exercise. Pentival is warm and passionate — he sees it as the most *fascinating* financial structure he's ever encountered. EyeQ keeps his knowledge as leverage. Pentival can't help sharing because the math is just so *interesting.*
