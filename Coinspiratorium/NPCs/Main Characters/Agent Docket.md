## Agent Docket
*Tactical Specialist / Enforcement*

Agent Docket is an inevitable — a construct of pure law, forged on the plane of Mechanus to enforce cosmic order. It has no personality. It has no opinions. It has **procedures**, and it executes them with absolute precision.

When O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. sends Quill, they want cooperation. When they send Docket, they've already decided cooperation has failed.

**Appearance:**
- Humanoid construct, seven feet tall, made of interlocking geometric plates of matte gray metal
- Face is a featureless oval with a single horizontal line that pulses faintly when they speak
- Moves with mechanical precision — no wasted motion, no idle gestures, no breathing
- Wears an O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E. badge pinned directly into its chest plate, as if someone told it "wear this" and it interpreted literally
- Heavy. When Docket steps onto a wooden floor, you hear it.

**Personality:**
- **None.** Docket does not have a personality. Docket has a directive.
- **Literal.** Processes language with zero subtext. Metaphors cause a brief pause.
- **Relentless.** Does not get tired, bored, frustrated, or discouraged. Does not speed up, slow down, or change course. Simply continues.
- **Polite, technically.** Uses "please" and "thank you" because those words are in their operational language pack. The politeness is more unsettling than any threat.
- **Not cruel.** Docket doesn't enjoy enforcement. Docket doesn't enjoy anything. Docket simply *completes the task.*

**Vibe:** A parking enforcement drone that can kick down a castle gate. The terminator if it filed paperwork. A walking subpoena.

**Sample Quotes:**
- *"You are non-compliant. This has been noted. Please comply."*
- *"I have been authorized to escalate. This is your notification."*
- *"Your emotional state has been registered. It does not affect the timeline."*
- *"Please do not run. Running adds a line item to your file."*
- *[long pause]* *"That was a metaphor. I have adjusted."*

**Role at the table:** Docket is the stick. When the PCs start thinking they can play O.P.E.R.A.T.I.V.E., Docket is the reminder that there are consequences. Docket should appear rarely — but when it does, the table should go quiet. Docket is also accidentally hilarious because of its total lack of personality creates absurd interactions with literally everyone.

**Contrast with EyeRate:** Both are enforcers. But EyeRate is rage — volatile, emotional, personal. Docket is *procedure* — cold, mechanical, utterly impersonal. EyeRate wants to hurt you. Docket doesn't want anything. It just has orders. Somehow that's worse.
