# Yorham Voss

Former wizard of the Arcane Brotherhood. Curator of the Archive of the First Knowing for thirty-one years, trapped by aboleth mucus that transformed his biology. Also known as Ilyara. Encountered in Quest 06.

## Role in the Archive Dungeon

Yorham is the PCs' primary social resource inside the 9-room archive. His helpfulness scales with how the PCs treat him during the Room 2 encounter:

### Graduated Helpfulness

| Relationship | How Earned | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Full ally** | Kind treatment + offered escape | Room-by-room warnings, trap DCs -2 throughout dungeon, provides Sorting Chamber passcode (Room 5), guides PCs through Memory Gauntlet one at a time (Room 6), reveals pedestal maintenance window (Room 9), offers Mnemon legal loophole unprompted |
| **Cooperative** | Polite, respectful | General layout info, warns about major hazards, describes Reading Pool function (Room 4) |
| **Minimal** | Dismissive or rude | "The pearl is deeper in." Nothing more |

### Key Dungeon Abilities

- **Sorting Chamber Passcode:** Yorham knows the coral-arm deactivation phrase for Room 5. Speaking it in Aboleth disables all six Sorting Arms immediately (the Mnemonic Ooze is unaffected — it's not part of the security system)
- **Gauntlet Guide (Room 6):** Yorham has walked the Memory Gauntlet thousands of times. He passes through it freely (the membrane recognizes him as staff). He can guide one PC at a time through the corridor, granting advantage on all three Will saves. He cannot guide the whole party at once — one at a time only
- **Reading Pool Calibration (Room 4):** If asked, Yorham calibrates the Reading Pool instantly (bypassing the Knowledge DC 18 check), enabling the PCs to preview the pearl's visions and reduce the cascade DC from 20 to 14 if the pearl is brought back to the pool
- **Pedestal Maintenance Window (Room 9):** Yorham knows the pedestal's psionic sensor cycles off for 6 seconds every 4 hours during a self-diagnostic. If PCs time the lift during this window, the alarm's Phase 1 countdown doubles from 60 seconds to 120 seconds
- **Mnemon Loophole:** If a full ally, Yorham whispers the Section 22/Subsection 3 academic privilege citation to the PCs before they even reach Room 9, giving them time to prepare their negotiation strategy
- **Tactical Intel (Combat):** During any combat encounter, a friendly Yorham telepathically provides tactical hints: weak points, behavioral patterns, and environmental advantages. This translates to +1 to attack rolls or skill checks once per encounter (DM's choice of when to offer it)

### The Moral Mirror

Yorham walks through the Memory Gauntlet freely (Room 6) — the membrane doesn't trigger on him because he has fully submitted to the archive's authority. The gauntlet forces PCs to relive rule-breaking, getting caught, and submitting. Yorham has already lived that arc. He IS the gauntlet's ideal outcome: a person who broke the rules, got caught, and submitted so completely that the system considers him part of itself.

The Aboleth Savant in Room 8 is Yorham's dark reflection — a scholar who was biologically reshaped into a search engine. Yorham retained his identity and autonomy; the Savant did not. If PCs visit Room 8 with Yorham, he goes very quiet. He recognizes what the Savant used to be. He recognizes what he could have become.

### Long-Range Contact

If befriended, Yorham has a method of long-range psionic communication (a gift from the aboleths). He contacts the PCs between adventures 7 and 8 with intelligence found in the archive — including detailed information about the Failed Crown's predecessor and its structural flaw (an unpaid promissory note that cascaded into total collapse).
