# Tesslyn Voss
*The impatient heir.*

> *"I wanted him to retire. Not die. There's a difference, and it matters."*

---

## Overview
[[Aldenmere Voss]]'s niece and only living relative. Has spent her adult life waiting for her uncle to die or retire. Sharp-featured, sharper-tongued, and calculating in a way that makes her look guilty even though she isn't.

## Appearance
- Human woman in her thirties
- Sharp features, dark eyes, formal dress
- Carries herself like someone who has been rehearsing authority her whole life

## Personality
- **Cold.** Not cruel — pragmatic. She doesn't waste emotion on situations she can't change.
- **Calculating.** Within minutes of her uncle's death, she's working the problem — estate, Instrument, will.
- **Honest about her ambition.** She wanted the inheritance. She didn't want the murder.

## Role
Red herring in [[11 - The Debt Collector's Wake]]. Strong suspect due to inheritance motive and cold demeanor. Has an airtight alibi (in conversation with another guest during the murder). Challenges the PCs' claim on legal grounds.

## Connections
- **Relationship to Voss:** Niece. Recently discovered her inheritance was reduced. This information was leaked by Veezy's network.
- **Relationship to Veezy:** She doesn't know he exists as anything more than a shopkeeper.

## Notes
- Her legal challenge to the PCs' claim (arguing Auric Holdings is a shell entity) is legitimate and should require effort to overcome
- She's not a villain. She's a woman who just lost her uncle and is processing it through the only lens she has: business
